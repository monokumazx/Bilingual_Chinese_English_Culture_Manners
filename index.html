礼仪核心概念与仪式

Core Etiquette & Ceremony — The foundation of manners, formalities, and traditional rituals.

礼仪 ⭐ START lǐyí etiquette; ceremony; propriety
中文释义社会生活中表示尊重和友好的行为规范和仪式。
English MeaningBehavioral norms and ceremonies that express respect and goodwill in social life.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 礼 + 仪 = 礼仪
礼 (lǐ) = ritual; courtesy; gift  |  仪 (yí) = ceremony; appearance; bearing
→ Courteous ceremony — etiquette.
📝 Example 中国是礼仪之邦。 China is a land of etiquette.
仪式 yíshì ceremony; ritual
中文释义按照一定程序进行的庄重活动。
English MeaningA solemn activity carried out according to a set procedure.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 仪 + 式 = 仪式
仪 (yí) = ceremony  |  式 (shì) = form; pattern
→ Ceremonial form.
📝 Example 毕业仪式让人难忘。 The graduation ceremony is unforgettable.
礼节 lǐjié etiquette; ceremonial courtesy
中文释义表示尊敬、祝贺、哀悼等的惯用形式。
English MeaningConventional forms used to express respect, congratulations, condolences, etc.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 礼 + 节 = 礼节
礼 (lǐ) = courtesy  |  节 (jié) = joint; segment; detail
→ Courteous details — etiquette.
📝 Example 握手是基本的礼节。 Shaking hands is a basic courtesy.
礼俗 lǐsú customs and etiquette
中文释义婚丧喜庆等场合的社会惯例与礼仪。
English MeaningSocial conventions and etiquette for occasions like weddings, funerals, and celebrations.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 礼 + 俗 = 礼俗
礼 (lǐ) = ritual  |  俗 (sú) = custom; folkway
→ Ritual customs.
📝 Example 不同地方有不同的礼俗。 Different places have different customs and etiquette.
风俗 fēngsú folk customs
中文释义社会上长期形成的风尚、礼节和习惯。
English MeaningLong-established social trends, etiquette, and habits.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 风 + 俗 = 风俗
风 (fēng) = wind; custom  |  俗 (sú) = folkway
→ Customs carried by the wind — folkways.
📝 Example 春节有很多有趣的风俗。 Spring Festival has many interesting folk customs.
隆重 lóngzhòng grand; solemn; ceremonious
中文释义盛大而郑重，表示对事情的高度重视。
English MeaningGrand and solemn, indicating high importance placed on the event.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 隆 + 重 = 隆重
隆 (lóng) = grand; prosperous  |  重 (zhòng) = serious; weighty
→ Grand and weighty — solemn.
📝 Example 婚礼举办得非常隆重。 The wedding was held very grandly.

礼貌与行为规范

Courtesy & Proper Conduct — Words that describe refined behavior and good manners.

举止 jǔzhǐ manners; bearing; demeanor
中文释义人的动作姿态和待人接物的风格。
English MeaningA person's posture, movements, and style in social interaction.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 举 + 止 = 举止
举 (jǔ) = to raise; behavior  |  止 (zhǐ) = to stop; demeanor
→ Behavior and bearing.
📝 Example 她的举止非常优雅。 Her manners are very elegant.
得体 détǐ appropriate; proper; fitting
中文释义言语行动恰到好处，符合场合和身份。
English MeaningSpeech and actions that are just right for the occasion and status.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 得 + 体 = 得体
得 (dé) = to obtain; to suit  |  体 (tǐ) = body; style; form
→ Fitting the form — appropriate.
📝 Example 她的穿着非常得体。 Her attire is very appropriate.
礼轻情意重 lǐ qīng qíngyì zhòng the gift is small but the sentiment is great
中文释义礼物虽不贵重，但代表的心意很深重。
English MeaningThe gift may be modest, but the thought behind it is profound.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 礼轻 + 情意 + 重 = 礼轻情意重
礼轻 (lǐ qīng) = gift is light  |  情意 (qíngyì) = affection  |  重 (zhòng) = heavy
→ A light gift, heavy sentiment.
📝 Example 这是我亲手做的点心，礼轻情意重，请收下。 These are homemade snacks; the gift is small but the thought is big. Please accept them.
入乡随俗 rù xiāng suí sú when in Rome, do as the Romans do
中文释义到一个地方就按照当地的风俗习惯行事。
English MeaningFollow the local customs wherever you go.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 入 + 乡 + 随 + 俗 = 入乡随俗
入 (rù) = enter  |  乡 (xiāng) = village  |  随 (suí) = follow  |  俗 (sú) = custom
→ Enter the village, follow the customs.
📝 Example 到了外国要入乡随俗。 When in a foreign country, do as the locals do.

迎送与寒暄

Greetings & Farewells — Welcoming guests and saying goodbye with warmth.

欢迎光临 ⭐ huānyíng guānglín welcome; we are honored by your presence
中文释义热情地迎接客人到来时使用的敬语。
English MeaningA polite phrase used to warmly welcome a guest's arrival.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 欢迎 + 光临 = 欢迎光临
欢迎 (huānyíng) = welcome  |  光临 (guānglín) = gracious presence (honorific)
→ Welcome, honored presence.
📝 Example 欢迎光临！里面请坐。 Welcome! Please come in and have a seat.
贵宾莅临 ⭐ guìbīn lìlín distinguished guest arrives; honored presence
中文释义尊贵的客人来到（正式场合用）。
English MeaningA distinguished guest arrives (used in formal settings).
🔍 Word Deconstruction 贵宾 + 莅临 = 贵宾莅临
贵宾 (guìbīn) = honored guest  |  莅临 (lìlín) = to arrive (honorific)
→ Honored guest graces us with arrival.
📝 Example 贵宾莅临，蓬荜生辉。 Your honored presence brings light to our humble home.
贵宾莅临，欢迎光临 ⭐ COMBO guìbīn lìlín, huānyíng guānglín Honored guests have arrived; welcome!
中文释义正式场合的完整欢迎语，先宣告贵宾到来，再表达热烈欢迎。
English MeaningA complete formal welcome phrase: first announcing the honored arrival, then warmly welcoming the guest.
🔍 Word Deconstruction 贵宾莅临 + 欢迎光临 = 贵宾莅临，欢迎光临
贵宾莅临 (guìbīn lìlín) = honored guest arrives  |  欢迎光临 (huānyíng guānglín) = welcome
→ Honored guests arrive — welcome!
📝 Example 会场门口挂着横幅：贵宾莅临，欢迎光临！ A banner at the venue entrance reads: "Honored guests have arrived — welcome!"
久仰 jiǔyǎng I've long admired you; pleased to meet you at last
中文释义对初次见面的人表示早就听说和仰慕的客气话。
English MeaningA polite phrase used when meeting someone for the first time, meaning "I've heard of you for a long time and admired you."
🔍 Word Deconstruction 久 + 仰 = 久仰
久 (jiǔ) = long time  |  仰 (yǎng) = to look up to; to admire
→ Long admired.
📝 Example 久仰大名，今日终于见面了。 I've long heard of your great name, and today we finally meet.

日常礼貌用语

Common Polite Expressions — Essential phrases for daily courtesy.

打扰了dǎrǎo leSorry to bother you
中文释义因自己的行为影响别人而表示歉意。
English MeaningApologizing for interrupting or troubling someone.
🔍 Word Deconstruction打 + 扰 + 了 = 打扰了
打 (dǎ) = to hit; to do  |  扰 (rǎo) = to disturb  |  了 (le) = completion
→ Have disturbed — sorry to bother.
📝 Example打扰了，请问现在几点了？Sorry to bother you, but what time is it?
不敢当bù gǎndāngI don't deserve such praise
中文释义谦虚地表示自己承受不起对方的称赞或待遇。
English MeaningModestly expressing that one does not deserve the compliment or treatment.
🔍 Word Deconstruction不 + 敢 + 当 = 不敢当
不 (bù) = not  |  敢 (gǎn) = dare  |  当 (dāng) = to bear; to accept
→ Dare not accept — you flatter me.
📝 Example您过奖了，不敢当！You overpraise me; I don't deserve it!
心领了xīn lǐng leI appreciate the thought
中文释义心里已经接受对方的好意，但婉言谢绝实际的好处。
English MeaningTo accept the goodwill in one's heart while politely declining the actual offer.
🔍 Word Deconstruction心 + 领 + 了 = 心领了
心 (xīn) = heart  |  领 (lǐng) = to receive  |  了 (le) = completion
→ Received in the heart — I appreciate the thought.
📝 Example这礼物我不能收，但你的好意我心领了。I can't accept this gift, but I truly appreciate the thought.
海涵hǎihánPlease forgive my shortcomings (formal)
中文释义像大海一样包容，请求别人原谅的客气话。
English MeaningLike the ocean's vast tolerance; a formal request for forgiveness.
🔍 Word Deconstruction海 + 涵 = 海涵
海 (hǎi) = sea  |  涵 (hán) = to contain; to forgive
→ Sea-like tolerance — please forgive.
📝 Example招待不周，还请海涵。The hospitality was lacking; please be magnanimous.

茶礼与待客

Tea Etiquette & Hospitality — Serving tea and receiving guests with grace.

奉茶fèngcháserve tea respectfully
中文释义用双手恭敬地端茶给客人。
English MeaningTo respectfully offer tea with both hands to a guest.
🔍 Word Deconstruction奉 + 茶 = 奉茶
奉 (fèng) = to offer respectfully  |  茶 (chá) = tea
→ Respectfully offer tea.
📝 Example新娘给公婆奉茶是婚礼的一部分。The bride serving tea to her in-laws is part of the wedding ceremony.
以茶待客yǐ chá dài kèreceive guests with tea
中文释义用茶来招待客人，是中国传统的待客之道。
English MeaningTo entertain guests with tea; a traditional Chinese way of hospitality.
🔍 Word Deconstruction以 + 茶 + 待 + 客 = 以茶待客
以 (yǐ) = with  |  茶 (chá) = tea  |  待 (dài) = to treat  |  客 (kè) = guest
→ Treat guests with tea.
📝 Example中国人讲究以茶待客。Chinese people value the tradition of serving tea to guests.
茶道chádàotea ceremony; the way of tea
中文释义通过沏茶、品茶来修身养性的艺术和仪式。
English MeaningThe art and ceremony of preparing and appreciating tea to cultivate oneself.
🔍 Word Deconstruction茶 + 道 = 茶道
茶 (chá) = tea  |  道 (dào) = way; path
→ The way of tea.
📝 Example日本的茶道深受中国文化影响。The Japanese tea ceremony was deeply influenced by Chinese culture.

餐桌礼仪

Table Manners — Dining etiquette and the language of shared meals.

公筷gōngkuàiserving chopsticks
中文释义用来夹取公用菜肴的筷子，与个人使用的筷子分开。
English MeaningChopsticks used for picking up shared dishes, separate from personal ones.
🔍 Word Deconstruction公 + 筷 = 公筷
公 (gōng) = public; shared  |  筷 (kuài) = chopsticks
→ Public chopsticks.
📝 Example为了卫生，请使用公筷。For hygiene, please use the serving chopsticks.
上座shàngzuòseat of honor
中文释义餐桌或会议室中最尊贵的位置，通常面对大门。
English MeaningThe most honorable seat at a table or meeting, usually facing the entrance.
🔍 Word Deconstruction上 + 座 = 上座
上 (shàng) = upper; superior  |  座 (zuò) = seat
→ The upper seat — seat of honor.
📝 Example请长辈坐上座。Please let the elder sit at the seat of honor.
动筷dòngkuàibegin eating; pick up chopsticks
中文释义宴席上开始用筷子夹菜的动作。
English MeaningThe act of starting to use chopsticks at a meal.
🔍 Word Deconstruction动 + 筷 = 动筷
动 (dòng) = to move  |  筷 (kuài) = chopsticks
→ Move chopsticks — start eating.
📝 Example等长辈先动筷我们才能开始吃。We wait for the elders to start eating before we begin.

婚礼与婚俗

Wedding & Marriage Customs — From proposals to traditional rituals.

三书六礼⭐ TRADITIONsān shū liù lǐThree Letters and Six Rites (traditional marriage process)
中文释义中国古代婚礼的完整程序，包括聘书、礼书、迎书以及纳采、问名、纳吉、纳征、请期、亲迎六个步骤。
English MeaningThe complete traditional Chinese wedding procedure, consisting of three letters and six ritual steps.
🔍 Word Deconstruction三 + 书 + 六 + 礼 = 三书六礼
三 (sān) = three  |  书 (shū) = letter  |  六 (liù) = six  |  礼 (lǐ) = rite
→ Three letters and six rites.
📝 Example三书六礼是中国传统婚礼的完整程序。The Three Letters and Six Rites represent the complete traditional Chinese wedding procedure.
拜堂bàitángwedding bows; formal ceremony of bowing
中文释义新郎新娘在婚礼上拜天地、拜父母和互拜的仪式。
English MeaningThe ceremony where the bride and groom bow to Heaven and Earth, parents, and each other.
🔍 Word Deconstruction拜 + 堂 = 拜堂
拜 (bài) = to bow  |  堂 (táng) = hall
→ Bowing in the hall.
📝 Example传统婚礼中拜堂是最重要的环节。In traditional weddings, the bowing ceremony is the most important part.
迎亲yíngqīnwelcoming the bride; fetching the bride
中文释义新郎到新娘家迎接新娘的仪式。
English MeaningThe ritual of the groom going to the bride's home to escort her.
🔍 Word Deconstruction迎 + 亲 = 迎亲
迎 (yíng) = to welcome  |  亲 (qīn) = relative; bride
→ Welcome the bride.
📝 Example迎亲的队伍非常热闹。The bride-fetching procession was very lively.

人生礼仪

Life Ceremonies — Customs marking milestones from birth to farewell.

抓周zhuāzhōufirst birthday object-picking ceremony
中文释义婴儿满周岁时，在盘中摆放各种物品让其抓取，以预测未来志向。
English MeaningA ceremony where a one-year-old child picks an object from a tray to foretell future inclinations.
🔍 Word Deconstruction抓 + 周 = 抓周
抓 (zhuā) = to grab  |  周 (zhōu) = year (full cycle)
→ Grabbing at one year old.
📝 Example宝宝抓周抓了一本书，大家说他将来会当学者。The baby grabbed a book during the ceremony; everyone said he would become a scholar.
成人礼chéngrénlǐcoming-of-age ceremony
中文释义庆祝年轻人长大成人的仪式。
English MeaningA ceremony celebrating a young person reaching adulthood.
🔍 Word Deconstruction成人 + 礼 = 成人礼
成人 (chéngrén) = adult  |  礼 (lǐ) = ceremony
→ Adult ceremony.
📝 Example学校为高三学生举行了成人礼。The school held a coming-of-age ceremony for the senior students.
节哀顺变jié'āi shùnbiànmy condolences; please restrain your grief
中文释义对遭遇不幸的人表示哀悼，并劝其节制悲伤、顺应变故。
English MeaningExpressing condolences and advising to restrain grief and accept the change.
🔍 Word Deconstruction节 + 哀 + 顺 + 变 = 节哀顺变
节 (jié) = restrain  |  哀 (āi) = grief  |  顺 (shùn) = follow  |  变 (biàn) = change
→ Restrain grief, follow the change.
📝 Example请节哀顺变，保重身体。Please accept my condolences and take care of yourself.

商务礼仪

Business Etiquette — Polite conduct in professional settings.

名片míngpiànbusiness card
中文释义在正式场合介绍身份和联系方式的小卡片。
English MeaningA small card used to introduce one's identity and contact information in formal settings.
🔍 Word Deconstruction名 + 片 = 名片
名 (míng) = name  |  片 (piàn) = card; slice
→ Name card.
📝 Example交换名片时要用双手递接。When exchanging business cards, use both hands to give and receive.
握手wòshǒuhandshake
中文释义见面或告别时彼此握住右手以示友好。
English MeaningGrasping each other's right hand upon meeting or parting to show friendliness.
🔍 Word Deconstruction握 + 手 = 握手
握 (wò) = to grasp  |  手 (shǒu) = hand
→ Grasp hands.
📝 Example初次见面，我们友好地握手。We shook hands warmly when we first met.

儒家礼文化

Confucian Ritual Culture — The classical foundations of Chinese etiquette.

五礼wǔlǐFive Categories of Rites
中文释义古代礼仪的五大类别：吉礼、凶礼、宾礼、军礼、嘉礼。
English MeaningThe five ancient categories of rites: auspicious, funeral, guest, military, and celebratory.
🔍 Word Deconstruction五 + 礼 = 五礼
五 (wǔ) = five  |  礼 (lǐ) = rite
→ Five rites.
📝 Example五礼是儒家礼仪的基本分类。The Five Rites are the basic classification of Confucian ritual.
《礼记》LǐjìBook of Rites
中文释义儒家经典之一，记录了古代礼仪制度和孔门弟子的言论。
English MeaningOne of the Confucian classics, recording ancient ritual systems and sayings of Confucius' disciples.
🔍 Word Deconstruction礼 + 记 = 《礼记》
礼 (lǐ) = rites  |  记 (jì) = record
→ Record of Rites.
📝 Example《礼记》对中国文化影响深远。The Book of Rites has profoundly influenced Chinese culture.
